Internship & Industrial Training

  1. Home
  2. Services



We are providing solutions to students who are seeking for industrial training and internship program to groom students for their better career. We are trying to fill gap between university and industries.

Our aim is to train students to work as an industrialist and to nourish and guide with their own ideas for their future career.



Who can apply?


B.Sc / M.Sc ( 1st / 2nd & 3rd year students )

Students who want to go for a perfect hands on expert training program with basics of biotechnology and microbiology which help them specialized in the specific microbiology and biotechnology fields.



Training modules


-> Industrial Microbiology

Duration 4 weeks

1.Good laboratory practice
2.Principle & working of laboratory instruments
3.Introduction to sterilization techniques
4.Introduction to Industrial Microbiology
5.Media preparation ( solid & liquid Media)
6.Dilution techniques of soil & water
7.Calculation of colony forming units ( CFU / ml)
8.Calculation of spore count using neubour's chamber.
9.Isolation of microorganisms on selective media. (by different plate techniques)
10.Preservation of microbial culture techniques
11.Basic biochemical characterization of isolated microorganisms.
12.Microbial Bioassay
13.Sub culturing techniques.
14.Standard operating procedure (SOP) and application of instruments (Autoclave, pH meter, Laminar air flow, incubator and microscope)


-> Molecular Biology

Duration 2 weeks

1.Good laboratory practice
2.Principle & working of laboratory instruments
3.Basic calculation techniques
4.Preparation of buffers and stock solutions
5.Isolation of genomic DNA by CTAB method
6.analysis of nucleic acid by gel electrophoresis
7.analysis of protein by SDS - PAGE
8.Preparation of TAE , agarose gel and loading buffer dye
9.Isolation of microorganisms on selective media.


-> Enzymology

Duration 4 weeks

1.Good laboratory practice
2.Principle & working of laboratory instruments
3.Introduction about enzyme technology
4.Introduction and application of commercially enzyme product by microorganisms.
5.Production of commercially important enzymes( lipase , protease , cellulase , amylase , pectinase)
6.Identification and screening of different enzyme producing microorganisms.
